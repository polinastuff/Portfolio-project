About

DOMNINA

Domnina is a techno dj from Narva. She studied classical music (violin and piano) and after that, electornic music at Edinburgh Napier University. As many people, she got interested in techno after visiting Berlin and attending events at legendary clubs 'Tresor' and 'Berghain'.

Since then, she started to search for records that reminded her of that atmosphere and every set of hers is an attempt to convey that feeling she first experienced back in 2013.
